Flowtide lets you freely customize the background of your New Tab page. To customize it, head to Settings, navigate to background, and choose your option.

## Options

#### Wallpaper

Get a beautiful image from our collection of \~170 images.

#### Color palette

See a solid color on every reload.

#### Gradient

See a curated gradient on every reload.

#### **Blank**

Just a blank, black background.
