---
title: 'Soundscapes'
description: 'Listen to calming soundscapes right in your New Tab.'
---

<Info>
  Soundscapes are disabled by default.
</Info>

Flowtide provides a feature where you can listen to calming soundscapes, provided by [Noisefill](https://www.noisefill.com). To enable it, head to Settings, navigate to Features, and toggle it on.
