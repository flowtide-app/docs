---
title: "To-do list"
description: "Learn about the built-in to-do list in Flowtide."
---

<Info>
  To-do list is disabled by default.
</Info>

Flowtide ships with a built-in to-do list to keep track of your todos. The feature comes with  a widget of your oldest unchecked to-do under the clock, as well as a separate menu for all of your todos. To enable it, head to Settings, navigate to Features, and enable To-do list. The to-do list is designed to be simple, with basic features like deleting, renaming, and hiding completed.
