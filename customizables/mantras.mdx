---
title: 'Mantras'
description: 'Inspiration on every reload.'
---

<Info>
  Flowtide comes with mantras enabled by default.
</Info>

Mantras are a great way to get inspiration whenever you open a new tab. Flowtide comes with 15 mantras by default, and you can enable them in the settings. To enable them, open Settings, go to the Features section, and toggle on mantras.
