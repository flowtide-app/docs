---
title: 'Fonts'
description: 'Customize the fonts in Flowtide.'
---

If you don't like the default Sans font, you can easily customize the font to your liking. To customize the font, open Settings, and navigate to the Font section.

### **Options**

**Basic fonts:**

* Sans

* Serif

* Monospace

**Extra fonts:**

* Times New Roman

* Verdana

* Brush Script MT

* Georgia
