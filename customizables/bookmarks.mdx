---
title: 'Bookmarks'
description: 'Easily access your bookmarks from Flowtide.'
---

<Info>
  Bookmarks are disabled by default.
</Info>

Flowtide provides a featured called Bookmarks. The feature adds a widget below the clock that displays your bookmarks (first 9 bookmarks) and a menu on the top-right corner. To enable it, head to Settings, navigate to Features, and switch the toggle.
