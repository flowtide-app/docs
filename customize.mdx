## Customization concepts

Flowtide is designed to be as minimal as you want it to be, while also being very customizable.

### Stuff you can customize

<CardGroup cols="1">
  <Card title="Theme" icon="circle-half-stroke">
    Customize your theme
  </Card>

  <Card title="Mantras" icon="quotes">
    Inspiration on every reload.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Font" icon="font">
    Choose your favorite font.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Wallpaper Refresh Time" icon="rotate-right">
    Choose how often you want a new wallpaper.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Background" icon="square">
    Choose whether you want your background to be a wallpaper, solid color, or gradient.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Clock format" icon="clock">
    Choose whether you want 12-hour or 24-hour time.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Clock size" icon="text-size">
    Choose how big you want your clock to be (if you have it enabled)
  </Card>

  <Card title="To-do list" icon="list">
    Keep track of your todos with a widget under the clock and a separate menu.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Bookmarks" icon="bookmark">
    Choose to get a grid of your bookmarks below the clock and a seperate menu.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Soundscapes" icon="volume">
    Listen to calming soundscapes right on your New Tab page.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

As you can see, there's so much to customize! We encourage you to dive deep so you can make your New Tab page the best it can be.
